About Us

____Welcome to Eato.fit____



Eato.fit, as the name suggests, is a popular quick service restaurant serving fresh and lip-smacking food. We serve a variety of dishes as well as an exhaustive list of items from our menu at very affordable prices. Our popular dish being Dal Fry & Dahi Bade is a crowd favourite. We also serve delicious Ghujiya and Churma, which has been pleasing many customers since the time of inception. Our staff is very courteous as well as hard working to please anyone who dines with us. Our outlet is located at Sarjapur road, you can also find us on Google for directions.
